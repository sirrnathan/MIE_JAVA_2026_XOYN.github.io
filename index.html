About Me

I'm Nceba Xoyiya, a software engineer and tech enthusiast. My journey into tech is driven by a deep curiosity about how systems work and a desire to build tools that make a real difference. I believe technology should serve people—not the other way around. That’s why I’m passionate about creating systems that automate repetitive tasks, streamline complex processes, and free up time for what truly matters: creativity, connection, and human growth.

With hands-on experience in full-stack development and embedded systems, I enjoy bridging the gap between software and hardware to solve practical problems. Beyond the code, I value professionalism, perseverance, and culture. I believe that great software is born not just from technical skill, but from collaboration, empathy, and a commitment to continuous learning. I approach every project with the mindset that we're not just building features—we're building solutions that matter.
